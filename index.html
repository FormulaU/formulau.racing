About Formula U

Formula U Racing is the Formula SAE team at the University of Utah.

Formula SAE is a student design and engineering competition organized by the SAE. Students from all around the world design and engineer experimental racecars that participate in a gauntlet of technical and racing challenges every year in Lincoln, Nebraska.

The U's team includes undergraduate and graduate students drawn from nearly every engineering discipline, as well as related majors. Our team is always looking for talented, driven students at the U that have what it takes to contribute to a engineering team.
